Grain to grind

Four stages, tracked from cup back to farm

Roasting is the one step we never outsource. Everything else follows from getting this stage right.

01

Green selection

Sample-roast and cup every lot before it's bought. Anything below our score threshold doesn't get a bag.

02

Roast profile

Each origin gets its own charted curve, timed to first crack, on a 12kg drum roaster.

03

Rest & degas

Beans rest 24–48 hours so CO2 escapes before sealing, protecting flavor for the trip to you.

04

Grind & brew

We grind to order for pickup, and ship whole bean so nothing goes stale in transit.
